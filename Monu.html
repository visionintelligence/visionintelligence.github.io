Monu Verma received her B. Tech degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Govt. Engineering College Bikaner, India, in 2013. She received her M. tech degree in 2016 from the National Institute of Technology Jalandhar, India. She is currently pursuing her Ph. D. under the supervision of Dr. Santosh Kumar Vipparthi and Dr. Girdhari Singh, at the Department of Computer Engineering, Malaviya National Institute of Technology Jaipur. She is currently working on the projects: 'Facial Expression Recognition' and 'Micro Expression Recognition'. She has been awarded by international fellowship "Shastri Research Student Fellowship (SRSF) Doctoral Award for the year 2018-19" at Concordia university Montreal, Canada under Prof. Nizar Bouguila. She is a invited reviewer for top ranking international journals (IEEE Transactions on Affective Computing, IEEE Transactions on Biometrics, Behavior, and Identity Science (T-BIOM), etc.)
