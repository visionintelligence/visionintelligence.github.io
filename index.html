About Us




Our Objective

The Vision Intelligence Lab is a multidisciplinary group performing basic and applied research computer vision, machine learning and deep learning. Our lab aims to develop intelligent algorithms that perform important visual perception tasks such as object detection, human emotion recognition, aberrant event detection, image retrieval, Motion analysis, etc.
