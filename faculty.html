Santosh Kumar Vipparthi is a Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Malaviya National Institute of Technology (MNIT), Jaipur (2013-Current). He was born in Komarada, Andhra Pradesh, India in 1985. He received his B.E. degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Andhra University, Andhra Pradesh, India, in 2007. Further, he received his M. Tech. and Ph. D. degrees in Systems Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Banaras Hindu University (IIT BHU), Varanasi, India, in 2010 and 2014 respectively.
